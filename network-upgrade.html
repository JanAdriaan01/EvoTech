What is a "full network upgrade"?

Most homes run on a single Wi-Fi router. A full network upgrade adds structured cabling, professional switches, and multiple access points – giving you enterprise-grade reliability at home.

Structured Cabling

Wired Ethernet ports in every room. Full 1Gbps – 10Gbps ready.

Managed Switches

Central hub that connects all your wired devices. PoE switches power cameras and access points.

VLAN Segmentation

Separate networks for family, guests, and IoT devices for better security.

What's included

  • Free site assessment – we plan cable routes and equipment placement
  • Cat6 cabling to each room (fished through walls, clean finish)
  • Wall plates and keystone jacks in every location
  • Central network rack or cabinet with patch panel
  • Enterprise-grade router (Ubiquiti / MikroTik)
  • PoE switch to power cameras and access points
  • Access Points (APs) for Wi-Fi coverage
  • VLAN configuration for security
  • 2-hour training session
