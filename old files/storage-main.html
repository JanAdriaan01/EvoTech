RAID Configuration Guide

Which RAID Should You Choose?

RAID LevelMinimum DrivesUsable SpaceProtectionBest For
RAID 0 2 100% None Speed (not recommended) Not Recommended
RAID 1 2 50% 1 drive failure Home users, critical data Best for Home
RAID 5 3 67-75% 1 drive failure Business, CCTV footage Best for Business
RAID 6 4 50-67% 2 drive failures Large businesses, archives Enterprise
RAID 10 4 50% 1-2 drive failures Speed + protection Performance

Not sure which RAID to choose? We'll assess your needs and recommend the best configuration.
