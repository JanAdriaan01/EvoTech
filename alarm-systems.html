Smart Alarm Features

Mobile App Control

Arm/disarm from anywhere. Real-time push notifications. System status at a glance.

Load Shedding Backup

8-12 hour battery backup. Alarm works during power outages. Cellular backup available.

Wireless Sensors

No drilling required. Door/window contacts, PIR motion, glass break sensors.

Cellular Backup

Alarm communicates via LTE if Wi-Fi is down. Perfect for load shedding and fibre outages.

Pet Immune Sensors

Motion sensors ignore pets up to 40kg. Your pets can move freely without triggering false alarms.

Entry/Exit Delays

Customizable delays. Time to arm/disarm without triggering the alarm.

Protection for every situation

At Home

Arm perimeter sensors (doors/windows) while staying inside. No motion sensors active – move freely without setting off the alarm.

Away

Full system armed – doors, windows, motion sensors, glass breaks. Instant mobile alert if anything triggers.

Night / Sleep

Perimeter armed + downstairs motion sensors. Upstairs area stays disarmed – safe movement at night.
