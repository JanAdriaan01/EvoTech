Fibre Optic Options

Single Mode Fibre

Longest distance (up to 40km+). Best for farms, agricultural holdings, and large properties. Immune to electrical interference.

Multimode Fibre

Up to 550m distance. Ideal for estates, schools, campuses, and office parks.

Armoured Fibre

Outdoor-rated with metal armour. Perfect for direct burial, trenching, and exposed runs.

Why Fibre for CCTV?

Long Distance

Fibre can transmit video signals up to 40km – much further than Ethernet (100m) or Wi-Fi.

No Interference

Immune to electrical interference, lightning strikes, grounding issues, and EMI/RFI.

Highly Secure

Cannot be tapped or intercepted without physical access. No signal radiation.

Future-Proof

Supports 4K, 8K, and future camera resolutions. Bandwidth up to 100Gbps+.

No Power Required

Fibre carries light, not electricity. Use PoE injectors or local power at camera end.

Reliable

No signal degradation over distance. Perfect for critical 24/7 security systems.

Compatible With All CCTV Systems

IP Cameras

Connect any IP camera via fibre media converter. Hikvision, Dahua, Hilook, Uniview, TP-Link.

Coax (Analogue) Cameras

Use coax-to-fibre converters for HD-TVI, HD-CVI, AHD, and CVBS cameras.

Solar CCTV

Perfect for off-grid solar cameras. Fibre carries data without draining battery.

PTZ Cameras

Full PTZ control over fibre. Perfect for farm perimeter monitoring.
