Farm Alarm Systems

Protect farmhouse, equipment sheds, fuel storage, and outbuildings.

  • Wireless sensors – no cabling across the farm
  • Long-range wireless (up to 2km between sensors and panel)
  • Mobile alerts – know instantly if a shed is breached
