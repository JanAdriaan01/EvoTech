About Net Cam SA

Your trusted partner for professional CCTV and network installations across Gauteng

Our Story

Net Cam SA was founded with a simple mission: to provide homeowners and businesses with professional, reliable CCTV and network installations without the confusing technical jargon or hidden fees.

What started as a small team of certified network engineers has grown into a trusted local business serving over 500 homes, farms, and offices across Midrand, Centurion, Pretoria, and Johannesburg.

We're a family-owned and operated business, and we take pride in being your neighbours – not a distant call centre. Every installation is done by our own certified technicians, and we stay until you're completely comfortable with your system.
