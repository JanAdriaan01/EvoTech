About Evo Home Tech

Your trusted partner for home networking, security, and farm solutions since 2018

Our Story

Evo Home Tech was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to provide homeowners and farmers with reliable, integrated networking and security solutions without the confusing technical jargon or hidden fees.

What started as a small team of networking engineers has grown into a trusted local business serving over 500 homes and farms across Midrand, Centurion, Pretoria, and Johannesburg.

We're family-owned and operated, and we take pride in being your neighbours – not a distant call centre. Every installation is done by our own certified technicians, and we stay until you're completely comfortable with your system.
