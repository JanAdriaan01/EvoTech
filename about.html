Our Story

Net Cam SA was founded with a simple mission: to provide homeowners, farmers, and businesses across Gauteng with professional, reliable CCTV and network installations without confusing technical jargon or hidden fees.

What started as a small team of certified network engineers has grown into a trusted local business serving over 500 homes, farms, and offices across President Park, Crowthorne, Vorna Valley, Waterfall, Kyalami, Carlswald, Midrand, Centurion, Pretoria, Johannesburg, Sandton, Fourways, and all Gauteng.

We specialize in IP CCTV with backup power, Coax CCTV, Solar CCTV, PTZ cameras, and video intercom systems. All our installations include UPS backup power for load shedding protection and a 2-year workmanship guarantee.

We're a family-owned and operated business, and we take pride in being your neighbours – not a distant call centre. Every installation is done by our own certified technicians, and we stay until you're completely comfortable with your system. We also provide free post-installation support and configuration assistance.
